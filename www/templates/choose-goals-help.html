Choose one or more achievable goals to concentrate on today. If you think you can only manage one simple goal today, that's ok. Just getting out of bed can be a huge achievement some days.

We recommend not choosing more than 3 goals per day - you want to make sure you can complete everything! But if you're feeling confident, go for it!

Tap a goal to select it. When you've chosen all the goals you want to do today, tap submit.
