Help

We know that everyone's experience of depression is different, so icons can be interpreted however you find most helpful. If you're stuck, here are some ideas:

  • Happy - Hopeful
  • Sad - Fearful
  • Proud - Grateful
  • Ashamed - Disappointed
  • Calm - Relaxed
  • Angry - Frustrated - Tense
  • Active - Inspired
  • Passive - Bored - Numb
